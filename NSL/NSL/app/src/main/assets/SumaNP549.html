Suma - NP 549

Paddy Research varieties

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Suma(NP - 549)
Plant Height: 95 - 100 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: Kharif: 120-125 days Rabi: 120-125 days
Grain Type: Long slender
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to BPH, BLB
Suitability: Suited for irrigated Rabi and late Kharif conditions
Areas of Adaptability: All states

Special Features/USPs:

  • High yielder
  • Coarse grain with high grain weight
  • Shorter duration, hence requires less water and suitable for double-cropped areas
  • High milling recovery
