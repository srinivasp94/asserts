Karishma - NPH 8899

Paddy Hybrids

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Karishma (NPH - 8899)
Plant Height: 90 - 95 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: 130 - 135 days (likely to be earlier by 5 days in Northern regions)
Grain Type: Medium slender, fine similar to BPT-5204
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, BPH and brown spot
Suitability: Suited for Kharif areas wherever BPT 5204/ Sambamahsuri is grown
Areas of Adaptability: Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Orissa, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu

Special Features/USPs:

  • Unique product with high yields and good grain quality
  • Fetches high market price due to fine grain
  • Good cooking quality
  • Tolerant to major diseases and pests
  • High milling recovery of >70%
