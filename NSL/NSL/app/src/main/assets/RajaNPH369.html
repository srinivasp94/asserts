Raja - NPH 369

Paddy Hybrids

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Raja (NPH 369)
Plant Height: 85-90 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: Kharif: 120 - 125 days (110 - 115 days in UP)
Grain Type: Long slender
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, brown spot
Suitability: Suited for both seasons in irrigated areas
Areas of Adaptability: Uttar Pradesh, Uttaranchal, Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Orissa and West Bengal

Special Features/USPs:

  • Short duration
  • Can be grown with limited irrigation
  • Suited for growing in both Kharif & Rabi
  • Non-lodging hybrid
