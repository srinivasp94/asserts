Sonal - NP 3114

Paddy Research varieties

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Sonal (NP - 3114)
Plant Height: 90 - 95 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: Kharif: 120 -125 days
(115 to 120 days in North Zone),
Rabi: 125 -130 days
Grain Type: Medium slender grains, similar to BPT-5204
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, brown spot, brown plant hopper
Suitability: Suited for all areas and for both seasons where BPT-5204 or Sonamahsuri is cultivated
Areas of Adaptability: Andhra Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Bihar, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Orissa, West Bengal, Karnataka, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh and Tamil Nadu

Special Features/USPs:

  • High yielder
  • Tolerant to major diseases and pests compared to BPT 5204
  • Fine grain, fetches high market price
  • Excellent cooking quality
  • Higher milling recovery
