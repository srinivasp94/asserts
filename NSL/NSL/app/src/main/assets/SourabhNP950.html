Sourabh - NP 950

Paddy Research varieties

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Sourabh(NP - 950)
Plant Height: 90 - 95 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: Kharif: 125-130 days Rabi: 130-135 days (120-125 days in Northern Region)
Grain Type: Long slender grains, husk-straw color
Rice: White colour
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, brown spot
Suitability: Suited for Kharif and Rabi in irrigated medium lands, for both Kharif, Rabi and for Boro seasons
Areas of Adaptability: Andhra Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Orissa, West Bengal, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Haryana and Punjab

Special Features/USPs:

  • Grain with high grain weight
  • Uniformity in maturity
  • High tillering and high yielder
  • Suitable to Kharif, Rabi and Boro seasons
  • Strong stem with non-lodging habit
