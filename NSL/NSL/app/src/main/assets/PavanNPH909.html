Pavan - NPH 909

Paddy Hybrids

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Pavan (NPH - 909)
Plant Height: 95 - 100 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: 108 - 112 days
Grain Type: Long slender
Rice: White
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, brown spot, BPH
Suitability: Suited for growing in Kharif & Rabi seasons
Areas of Adaptability: Irrigated areas of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Jharkhand, West Bengal and Orissa

Special Features/USPs:

  • Shorter duration
  • High grain weight and good tillering
  • Excellent uniformity
  • Drought tolerant and also performs in saline soil
