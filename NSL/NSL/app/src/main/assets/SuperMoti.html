Super Moti

Paddy Research varieties

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Super Moti
Plant Height: 90 - 95 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: Kharif: 125-130 days (115 - 120 days in Northern Region), Rabi: 130 - 135 days
Grain Type: Medium slender grains, husk-straw colour
Rice: White colour
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, brown spot, brown plant hopper
Suitability: Suited for irrigated, low or medium lands, for both Kharif Rabi and Boro seasons
Areas of Adaptability: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Orissa, West Bengal, Bihar, Jharkhand and Uttar Pradesh

Special Features/USPs:

  • High yielder
  • Tolerant to major diseases and pests
  • Fine grain, fetches high market price
  • Excellent cooking quality
  • High milling recovery
