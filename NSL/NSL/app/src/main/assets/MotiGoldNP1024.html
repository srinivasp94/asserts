Moti Gold - NP 1024

Paddy Research varieties

Morphological Characters
Hybrid Character: Shaan - NP 124
Plant Height: 90-95 cm, semi-dwarf
Maturity: Kharif: 130 - 135 days Rabi: 135-140 days
Grain Type: Medium slender grains, husk-straw colour
Rice: White colour
Disease/Pest Tolerance: Tolerant to blast, brown spot, brown plant hopper
Suitability: Suited for irrigated, low or medium lands
Areas of Adaptability: Andhra Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Orissa, West Bengal, Bihar, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka and Tamil Nadu

Special Features/USPs:

  • High yielder
  • Fine grain, fetches high market price
  • Excellent cooking quality and taste
  • High milling recovery
